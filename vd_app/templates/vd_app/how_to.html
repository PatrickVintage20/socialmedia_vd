How to Use the Video Downloader

Follow these simple steps to download videos from various platforms:

  1. Copy the video URL from Facebook, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, or Twitter.
  2. Paste the URL into the input field on the homepage.
  3. Click the "Download" button.
  4. Your video will be ready to download!
